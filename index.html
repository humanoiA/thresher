About us

Let Us Tell You Our Story

Haryana Agriculture works is one the top leading manufacturers and suppliers of agriculture Threshers. Since our establishment in 1987, we are providing the highest quality Threshers which are known for their heavy duty operation capability, durability and excellent performance. These heavy duty Threshers provide longer service life without any breakdowns which makes them really cost effective farm equipment solution. Our experienced team which is lead by Mr. Om Prakash Nokhwal since 1987, is well trained & dedicated to provide the farmers with best in class quality of Thresher Machines in our state of art manufacturing facility. Our motto is to bring prosperity in life of farmers by providing them farming equipment solutions which are hassle free and having long service life.
